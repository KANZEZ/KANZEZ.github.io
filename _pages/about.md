I'm a second-year master student at department of Mechanical Engineering and Applied Mechanics (MEAM), University of Pennsylvania. Currently I do research at GRASP Lab, supervised by Prof. Vijay Kumar. I received my Bachelor degree of Robotics Engineering at Southern University of Science and Technology, China.

My research interests lies in the intersection of planning, control and learning, I'm deeply interested in motion planning, UAV,  optimization and deep learning, my future works may lie in risk-aware navigation, multi-agent reinforcement learning, LLM and task allocation for heterougenous team.
